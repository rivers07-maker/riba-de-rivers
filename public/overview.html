Our cozy apartment 10 minutes walk from the beach is perfect for a getaway as a couple, with friends or family. Sleeps 4, it has a room, full bathroom, equipped kitchen, living room with sofa bed, private terrace and parking for cars and bicycles.

Enjoy all that the east coast of Asturias has to offer. Beautiful beaches, incredible landscapes, sports activities, and the best gastronomy.
